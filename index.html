Where the Heck Am I?

Welcome to the game that begs the question, "Where the heck am I?" Players will arrive at their randomly chosen destination and will have a limited amount of time to figure out where the heck they are. The player who makes the most accurate guess in the shortest amount of time wins! So what the heck are you waitin' for! Go get lost, why doncha?!
